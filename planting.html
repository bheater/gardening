Planting

Supplies

  • Plant
  • Pot (twice the size of the plant)
  • Potting soil
  • Gardening gloves
  • Hand shovel
  • Watering can with water

Potting a Plant

  1. Use the hand shovel to fill the bottom third of the pot with potting soil.
  2. Remove the plant from its container by pressing in the sides of the container and loosesing up the soil. Pinch in the corners of the container. If neccessary, use scissors to cut the plastic container to make it easier to transplant to the new pot.
  3. Add soil to the pot to fill in around the sides of the plant. Continue to add soil on top of the tranferred plant, until the soil falls 1 inch below the top of the pot.
  4. Water the plant daily for the first few weeks.
